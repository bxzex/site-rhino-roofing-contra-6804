Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer warranties on commercial roofs?

Yes, we offer comprehensive warranties. Depending on the system installed, we provide manufacturer No Dollar Limit (NDL) warranties ranging from 10 to 30 years, alongside our own workmanship guarantee.

Can you install a new roof without disrupting our business?

Absolutely. We specialize in occupied commercial building reroofing. We coordinate closely with your management team to ensure safety, minimize noise, and maintain zero operational downtime.

What is the best roofing system for a flat commercial building?

It depends on your specific needs, budget, and climate. TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) is highly popular for its energy efficiency and durability. EPDM is excellent for longevity. We will assess your building and recommend the optimal solution.

Do you handle emergency roof repairs?

Yes, we offer rapid response emergency repair services for commercial properties to mitigate water damage and secure your facility quickly.
